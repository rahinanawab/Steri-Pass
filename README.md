# Steri-Pass

# 🏥 Smart Medical Tray Sterilization Management System

**Track, manage, and monitor medical trays with secure sterilization workflows.**

Steri-Pass is a hospital sterilization management platform designed to streamline the tracking and management of medical trays throughout the sterilization lifecycle. The system enables hospitals, vendors, and administrators to efficiently manage tray records, sterilization status, expiry dates, and complete traceability through QR-based identification and IoT-enabled automation.

## ✨ Features

- 🏥 Hospital sterilization management
- 📦 Medical tray registration and tracking
- ⚖️ Tray weight recording and monitoring
- 📅 Expiry date management
- 🔄 Sterilization status tracking
- 🔗 Unique QR code generation for each tray
- 📱 QR-based tray identification and traceability
- 🖨️ IoT-enabled QR scanner and printer integration
- 👥 Role-based Admin, Hospital, and Vendor workflows
- 💳 In-App Purchase (IAP) subscription management
- 📧 Automated payment confirmation emails and notifications

## 📱 About the Platform

Steri-Pass is built to improve medical tray management by providing a centralized system for hospitals and vendors. Each tray is digitally tracked from registration to checkout, ensuring accurate records, improved operational efficiency, and complete visibility throughout the sterilization process.

The platform uses QR code technology to maintain tray traceability. During checkout, staff members authenticate by scanning their personal QR code, then scan the tray QR code. The connected IoT printer automatically generates and prints the tray label containing all required details.

The system supports:

- Hospital sterilization teams
- Medical equipment vendors
- Hospital administrators
- Operations managers
- Healthcare organizations

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Admin creates and manages hospitals through the admin panel.
2. Hospital managers are invited and assigned access.
3. Staff members add medical trays with weight and expiry details.
4. Each tray receives a unique QR code.
5. Staff scans their personal QR code for authentication.
6. Tray QR code is scanned during checkout.
7. IoT printer automatically prints the tray label.
8. Tray status and details are tracked throughout the sterilization process.
